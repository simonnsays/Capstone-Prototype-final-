Connecting Components With Wires

Now we dive in to plugging each wire for each component either for power or data connection.

There are two types of wires in the computer: one for data connection to the ROM, and the other for power supply to essential components like the GPU, ROM, CPU, and Motherboard. Additionally, there's a connection for the front panel, encompassing the power button, USB ports, and occasionally including a LED light indicators, reset button, and 3.5mm audio jack ports.

To connect the wires simply open the wires inventory and drag the wires to their respective slots
